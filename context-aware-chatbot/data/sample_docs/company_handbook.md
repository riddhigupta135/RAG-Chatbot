# Company Employee Handbook

Welcome to our company! This handbook contains important information about our policies and procedures.

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
All full-time employees are eligible for remote work after completing their 90-day probationary period. Remote work arrangements must be approved by your direct manager and HR.

### Requirements
- Maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet connection
- Be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM in your local timezone)
- Attend all required virtual meetings with camera on
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during working hours

### Equipment
The company provides:
- Laptop computer
- Monitor (upon request)
- $500 home office stipend for first-time remote workers

## Time Off Policy

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- All employees receive 20 days of PTO per year
- PTO accrues at 1.67 days per month
- Unused PTO carries over up to 5 days to the next year
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance

### Sick Leave
- 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- No advance notice required
- Doctor's note required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days

### Parental Leave
- 12 weeks of paid parental leave for primary caregivers
- 4 weeks of paid parental leave for secondary caregivers
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth or adoption

## Security Guidelines

### Data Handling
All employees must follow these guidelines when handling company data:

1. **Confidential Data**: Customer PII, financial records, trade secrets
   - Never share via unsecured channels
   - Use company-approved encryption tools
   - Report any suspected breaches immediately

2. **Internal Data**: Internal documents, meeting notes, project plans
   - Share only with relevant team members
   - Use company Google Drive or SharePoint

3. **Public Data**: Marketing materials, press releases, job postings
   - Can be shared externally with manager approval

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Change every 90 days
- Never reuse passwords across systems
- Use the company password manager (1Password)

### VPN Usage
- VPN must be connected when accessing company resources
- Download VPN client from IT portal
- Contact IT support if connection issues persist

## Benefits Overview

### Health Insurance
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Company pays 80% of premiums
- Coverage starts on first day of employment
- Open enrollment in November

### 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Company matches 4% of salary
- Immediate vesting
- Enrollment available quarterly

### Professional Development
- $2,000 annual learning budget
- Access to LinkedIn Learning
- Conference attendance (with manager approval)
